# Agile Manifesto

Keep it simple. Deliver value early. Learn from change.

## Four Values

- Individuals and interactions over processes and tools
- Working software over comprehensive documentation
- Customer collaboration over contract negotiation
- Responding to change over following a plan

## Principles Most Relevant Here

- Satisfy the customer through early and continuous delivery of valuable software
- Welcome changing requirements, even late in development
- Business people and developers work together daily
- Simplicity: maximize the amount of work not done

## How Product Owner Tickets Should Reflect This

- Describe the user outcome before the implementation
- Write acceptance criteria as observable behavior
- Keep stories small enough to estimate and deliver
- Leave room for learning instead of over-specifying the solution
